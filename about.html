About Us

Sanjivani Cancer Centre
FOR GETTING BEST MEDICAL SERVICES

Sanjivani Cancer Centre was founded by young & dynamic leading Oncosurgeons of Maharashtra to provide complex and cutting-edge oncological surgeries & treatment at affordable prices across hospitals in Mumbai, Thane.

Surgeons from Sanjivani Cancer Centre have a vast experience in Laparoscopic or Key hole cancer surgery, Precision based robotic cancer surgery, Complex oral cancer with, Oncoplastic breast surgery and Intraperitoneal chemotherapy for advanced peritoneal cancer, truly bringing excellence to cancer care services in the region.
Cancer incidence in India is gradually rising with more than 1 million new cases being diagnosed in 2018 and is expected to double in the coming years.
