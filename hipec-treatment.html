HIPEC Surgery: A Breakthrough Cancer Treatment

HIPEC (Hyperthermic Intraperitoneal Chemotherapy) is an advanced procedure used to treat cancers within the abdominal cavity. It involves delivering heated chemotherapy directly inside the abdomen after surgically removing visible tumors.

How Does HIPEC Work?

After removing cancerous tumors, a heated chemotherapy solution is circulated within the abdominal cavity. The heat enhances the drug's effectiveness, allowing better penetration into tissues and killing remaining cancer cells without damaging healthy organs.

Advantages of HIPEC Treatment

HIPEC targets cancer locally, minimizes side effects compared to traditional chemotherapy, improves survival rates, and offers new hope for patients with advanced peritoneal cancers like ovarian, colon, and appendiceal cancers.
